If you were a boat, what type would you be?

As a reflection of my personality, I see myself as a sailboat. The freedom of sailing resonates with my adventurous spirit, while the reliance on the wind's direction reflects my belief in going with the flow of life.

"The pessimist complains about the wind; the optimist expects it to change; the realist adjusts the sails." - William Arthur Ward
