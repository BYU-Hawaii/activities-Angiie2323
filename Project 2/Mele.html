About Place 2

Welcome to Mele Village, a captivating gem nestled along the shores of Efate Island in Vanuatu. This charming community invites you to experience the rich tapestry of Ni-Vanuatu culture and hospitality. Immerse yourself in traditional ceremonies, vibrant dances, and the warm embrace of the local community. Surrounded by lush tropical landscapes and crystal-clear waters, Mele Village is not only a cultural hub but also a gateway to natural wonders like the Mele Cascades Waterfall and the Hideaway Island Marine Reserve. Join us on a journey of discovery and connection as we explore the beauty and spirit of Mele Village in Vanuatu. Welcome to an unforgettable adventure.
