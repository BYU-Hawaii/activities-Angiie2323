Welcome to Mele Village, a captivating gem nestled along the shores of Efate Island in Vanuatu. This charming community invites you to experience the rich tapestry of Ni-Vanuatu culture and hospitality. Immerse yourself in traditional ceremonies, vibrant dances, and the warm embrace of the local community. Surrounded by lush tropical landscapes and crystal-clear waters, Mele Village is not only a cultural hub but also a gateway to natural wonders like the Mele Cascades Waterfall and the Hideaway Island Marine Reserve. Join us on a journey of discovery and connection as we explore the beauty and spirit of Mele Village in Vanuatu. Welcome to an unforgettable adventure.

Situated off the coast of Mele Village on the island of Efate in Vanuatu, Hideaway Island and Mele Bay together create a captivating destination that blends natural beauty with exciting activities. Hideaway Island, renowned for its pristine waters, vibrant coral reefs, and the world's only underwater post office, offers exceptional snorkeling and diving experiences. Visitors can explore its marine sanctuary, swim, and send waterproof postcards from beneath the waves. Just a short boat ride away, Mele Bay complements this adventure with its tranquil, clear waters ideal for kayaking, paddleboarding, and relaxing on serene beaches. This combined location provides a perfect mix of underwater exploration and leisurely seaside enjoyment, all within a stunning tropical setting.
