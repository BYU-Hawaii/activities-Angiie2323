VANUATU'S FAVOURITE PLATE

Laplap is a traditional dish from Vanuatu made with grated root vegetables, coconut milk, and often meat or fish. It's wrapped in banana leaves and cooked over hot stones, resulting in a delicious and hearty meal. This dish is a symbol of community and tradition, often prepared and shared during special occasions. Laplap's rich flavors and cultural significance make it a must-try experience for visitors to Vanuatu.
